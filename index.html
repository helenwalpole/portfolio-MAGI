Hello, I'm Helen Walpole.

This portfolio site shows some examples of recent work, for an application for entry to RMIT Master of Animation, Games and Interactivity.

Dino discovery, 2021

A simple, name-generator game for indecisive paleontologists.

All my own work, using iOS app developed in Swift & XCode, an assignment for RMIT Online app development course.

Statue tour app, 2021

A walking tour app, with location-specific text-to-speech and push notifications, available in the app store.

I was employed by ESRI Inc to conceptualise, storyboard, develop and test this app. I used an ESRI code template and developed all content.

City DNA, 2023

A hybrid digital/physical interactive map, used for spatial data storytelling.

I manage this exhibit in Melbourne Town Hall, and am responsible for UX, content and code development, and collaborate on the cartography.

Melting Melbourne, 2020

An interacitve map encouraging playful data exploration.

All my own work, constructed using Mapbox GL JS.
An assignment for RMIT Grad Dip Geospatial Science, winner of NACIS student dynamic map award 2021.

Turf Wars, 2020

An interactive map exploring just how football-mad Australian are.

All my own work, constructed using Mapbox GL JS.
An assignment for RMIT Grad Dip Geospatial Science, optimised for Google Chrome.

Anzac Day, 2020

A printed map and data visualisation exploring the movement of people around Melbourne on Anzac Day.

All my own work, using ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator. An assignment for RMIT Master of Geospatial Science.

Atlas of the ACT, 2023

Commissioned series of 25 maps for a multi-volume history of the ACT region by James MacDonald.

I was responsible for all cartography and graphic design. I used ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator, using data from the author.

Australian Sports Museum, 2020

A new museum of Australia's Sporting Culture.

I was Creative Director of the museum redevelopment team, responsible for the curatorial concept, content development, play development and some exhibition design.

Inside the Museum, 2020

A series of short storytelling videos about objects in the musuem.

Mascot Matchup, 2020

A looping quiz game about Aussie sport mascots for the Australian Sports Museum.

I was responsible for concept, storyboard and gameplay, not the illustration, animation or programming.

Feral Map, 2021

A website about the non-human inhabitants of a city, with real-time submission of new content by users.

I was employed by RMIT to co-design this webmap, and responsible for full-stack development with Mapbox GL JS and Wordpress.
